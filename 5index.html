Who we are

We are a small team of plant enthusiasts based in London. We know the struggle of constantly buying new plants and doing our best to keep them alive but still needing to go back to the store for a new one withing a few months. We are here to take the hocus-pocus out ofof plants and flowers and teaching you simple techniques to understand a plant's needs in order to know where to place and how to style them, but most importantly how to keep them alive. We don't have a Phd in Botany but we learned from our own mistakes and we are here to teach you too.
